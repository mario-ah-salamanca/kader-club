# Kader Club

Kader Club is a football-card collection management app for European collectors.

The product starts as a trusted **digital binder and portfolio dashboard** before expanding into trade discovery, premium collector tools, and future playful layers such as Fantasy XI.

## Product Positioning

**Kader Club is the Transfermarkt for football cards:** a portfolio tracker, digital binder, and collector network for football-card collectors.

## MVP Goal

Build the first collection loop:

1. User signs up.
2. User completes a profile.
3. User searches a curated football-card catalog.
4. User opens a card detail page.
5. User adds the card to their collection.
6. User sees it in the digital binder/dashboard.
7. User can mark cards as open to trade.

## Non-Goals for MVP

The MVP must not become a marketplace.

Out of scope for v1:

- Payments
- Escrow
- Checkout
- Shipping workflows
- Physical authentication
- Complex negotiation tooling
- Public forums
- Complex social feeds

## North Star

Weekly active collectors who update their collection, wishlist, or trade status.

## Activation Metric

A user adds at least 10 cards and creates at least 1 wishlist item.
