# Approve exact MVP scope and non-goals

## Goal

Confirm what Kader Club MVP includes and explicitly excludes.

## Context

The MVP must validate Kader Club as a trusted digital binder and portfolio dashboard before marketplace workflows are considered.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] MVP includes catalog, collection, valuation, wishlist foundation, and trade discovery.
- [ ] MVP excludes marketplace workflows.
- [ ] Non-goals are documented in `README.md` and `docs/requirements.md`.
- [ ] Team can describe what can and cannot be done in v1.

## Labels

`type: decision`, `priority: P0`, `area: product`, `status: needs-decision`
